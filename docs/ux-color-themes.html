wedX – Color Theme Explorer

Conceptual palettes to guide the wedX Tailwind/shadcn theme setup. These are reference themes rather than final brand decisions.

Theme 1 – Calm Coordinator

Primary experience theme for planning views (Today, Checklist, Budget).

primary #ec4899
primary-soft #f9a8d4
support #0f766e
accent #fbbf24

- Calm neutrals with a warm accent for key actions.
- Ideal for daily use without visual fatigue.

  • Use in: Today, Checklist, Events, Budget
  • Primary buttons: #ec4899 on white
  • Backgrounds: neutral-50 to neutral-100

Theme 2 – Playful Celebration

Brighter, more expressive palette for inspiration-heavy surfaces.

primary #e11d48
accent #f97316
success #22c55e
info #0ea5e9

- Higher contrast and saturation for celebratory screens.
- Works well for Inspiration, AI Planner highlights, success moments.

  • Use sparingly to avoid visual overload
  • Success toasts and celebration states

Theme 3 – Luxe Evening

Darker, more luxurious palette suitable for night-mode or marketing.

bg #0b1120
surface #1f2937
accent-gold #facc15
accent-warm #f97316

- Luxurious feel for future dark theme or marketing pages.
- Not required for initial implementation but sets direction.

  • Use in: future dark mode, marketing landers
  • Requires careful contrast checks for AA/AAA
